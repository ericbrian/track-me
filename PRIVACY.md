# Privacy Policy - TrackMe

**Last Updated:** November 9, 2025

## Overview

TrackMe is a privacy-first GPS tracking application designed to give you complete control over your location data. This privacy policy explains how TrackMe handles your information.

## Our Privacy Commitment

**Your location data never leaves your device.** TrackMe is built on the principle of local-first data storage with zero data transmission to external servers.

## Data Collection

### Location Data

TrackMe collects your GPS location data for the following purposes:

- Recording your movement paths and trips
- Displaying your current position
- Calculating trip statistics (distance, duration, speed)
- Generating location history maps

### What We DON'T Collect

- **No personal information** - No name, email, or phone number required
- **No usage analytics** - We don't track how you use the app
- **No crash reports** - No automatic error reporting
- **No advertising data** - No ad tracking or third-party analytics
- **No network transmission** - Your data never leaves your device

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

All location data is stored exclusively on your device using:

- iOS Core Data framework
- Local device storage
- No cloud sync or backup. If you want a backup then you have to use the export function to create the backup.

### Data Access

Only you can access your location data through:

- The TrackMe app interface
- iOS system backups (if enabled)
- Device file system (with appropriate access)

## Location Permissions

### Why "Always Allow" is Required

TrackMe requests "Always Allow" location permission to enable:

- Continuous tracking during trips
- Background location updates when the app is not active
- Complete trip recording from start to finish

### Your Control

You maintain complete control:

- Enable/disable tracking at any time
- Revoke location permissions in iOS Settings
- Delete tracking sessions individually
- Export your data before deleting

## Data Retention

### User-Controlled Retention

- Data is retained until you delete it
- No automatic deletion or expiration
- Complete sessions or individual location points can be removed

### App Deletion

Deleting the TrackMe app from your device will permanently remove all location data. There is no way to recover this data as it exists only on your device.

## Data Sharing

**TrackMe does not share your data with anyone.**

Your location data:

- Is not transmitted over the internet
- Is not shared with third parties
- Is not sold or monetized
- Is not used for advertising
- Is not stored on remote servers

## Data Export

You can export your location data in the following formats:

- CSV (Comma-Separated Values)
- GeoJSON (Geographic JSON)

Exported data can be:

- Saved to your device
- Shared via iOS Share Sheet
- Imported into other applications

## Children's Privacy

TrackMe does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. No personal information is collected from any user regardless of age.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We will update this privacy policy if our data practices change. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any modifications.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access all your location data through the app
- Delete any or all of your data at any time
- Export your data in standard formats
- Stop location tracking at any time
- Uninstall the app and remove all data

## Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact:

- GitHub: <https://github.com/ericbrian/track-me>

## Technical Details

### Data Security

- Data is protected by iOS device encryption
- No network transmission means no transmission vulnerabilities
- Access controlled by iOS app sandboxing

### Third-Party Services

TrackMe does not integrate with or use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

### Open Source

TrackMe is open source. You can review the complete source code to verify our privacy claims at:
<https://github.com/ericbrian/track-me>

---

**Summary:** TrackMe is designed with your privacy as the top priority. Your location data is yours alone, stored only on your device, and never transmitted or shared. You maintain complete control over your data at all times.
